Introduction & Background

I am a Ph.D. student with a research focus in musculoskeletal biomechanics at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. My research interests involve experimental biomechanics and modeling, as well as the development of intraoperative sensors to enhance surgical procedures, such as total knee arthroplasty. I am currently working on a project involving the evaluation of a novel shear wave tensiometer to assess ligament tension during a TKA procedure.
